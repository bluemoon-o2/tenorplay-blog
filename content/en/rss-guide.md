### What is RSS?
RSS (Really Simple Syndication) is a technology that allows you to get the latest updates from websites without having to visit them repeatedly. You can subscribe to our blog through RSS readers like Feedly, Inoreader, or NetNewsWire.

### How to Subscribe?

1. **Copy the RSS feed link**:
  ```
  https://blog.tensorplay.cn/en/index.xml
  ```

2. **Add to reader**:
  Select "Add Subscription" or "+", then paste the link.

### Recommended Readers
- **Online Platforms**: [Feedly](https://feedly.com), [Inoreader](https://www.inoreader.com)
- **Apple Ecosystem**: [NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com), [Reeder](https://reederapp.com)
- **Windows**: [Fluent Reader](https://github.com/yang991178/fluent-reader)
- **Browser Extension**: [RSS Feed Reader](https://rssfeedreader.com)
